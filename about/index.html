About STOUT General Contracting

At STOUT General Contracting, our goal is to have satisfied clients. We understand it's more than just four walls and a roof, it's your home. With over 15 years of experience in residential and commercial construction, we deliver exceptional results that exceed expectations.

Our founder, Shane Stout, brings expertise and integrity to every project. We specialize in custom home construction, renovations, and commercial projects throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth area. Quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.
