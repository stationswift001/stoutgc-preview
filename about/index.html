About Stout General Contracting

Stout GC is a family-owned company established in 2010 by Shane Stout. Since 2015, we have specialized in high-end residential new construction in the Dallas area. Shane brings over 25 years of construction expertise, including cost-effective renovations, large-scale custom homes, high-end multi-family projects, and commercial finish-outs. This diverse experience has refined our processes to deliver exceptional quality while prioritizing budget management.
